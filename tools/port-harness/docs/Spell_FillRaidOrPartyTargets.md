# Spell::FillRaidOrPartyTargets

**File:** src/game/Server/Packets/Spell.cpp

## Summary
Spell::FillRaidOrPartyTargets appends eligible party or raid members (and optionally their pets) to TagUnitMap by resolving a player from the input Unit, iterating group members when grouped or falling back to a single owner/self unit, applying subgroup/raid scope, level, hostility, caster-inclusion, and in-map distance filters.
